Attention vs Engagement

Trying to separate the moment someone pauses from the moment they actually care.

3 min read

I am trying to separate attention from engagement in my own head.

Attention is the pause.

Engagement is the response.

A post can make someone stop because it is surprising, but that does not mean they care. A post can get comments because people are arguing, but that does not mean the brand became stronger.

That difference feels important.

The kind of work I want to understand better is the work that gets the pause and then gives people something worth doing with it.
